How I Work

A practical delivery system for web projects.

The goal is simple: make the work visible, make ownership clear, and keep the project moving without surprises.

01

Brief

Clarify goals, audience, stakeholders, success criteria, and what the client actually needs delivered.

02

Requirements

Turn notes into page scope, CMS needs, integrations, content gaps, and acceptance points.

03

Planning

Break the work into owners, dependencies, milestones, QA checkpoints, and delivery dates.

04

Coordination

Follow up across design, SEO, development, content, and account teams so blockers surface early.

05

Delivery

Track revisions, test functionality, confirm responsiveness, and prepare the site for a clean launch.

06

Handover

Close with SOPs, Loom walkthroughs, CMS notes, and client-ready documentation.
